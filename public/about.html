Our Story

Football Weekn'd Kicker started as a simple idea — a group of mates who just wanted to play football every weekend without the hassle of finding a pitch or coordinating on WhatsApp.

What began as 8 players on a muddy Sunday morning has grown into a community of 40+ registered members, all sharing the same love for the game.

No contracts. No politics. Just football.
